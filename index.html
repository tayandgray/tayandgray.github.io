Schedule of Events
Thursday Evening
We will be hosting a welcome party for guests that would like to join us early. We will be at Churchill Manor bed and breakfast from 5 - 7 PM. The bed and breakfast is located just a 3 minute walk from the above mentioned hotels, at 485 Brown Street, Napa CA, 94559. Direct line is (707)-253-7733. It will be a very casual evening, so feel free to come directly from your flight! Come hungry and thirsty, we can’t wait to kick off the weekend with you :)

Your day to enjoy all that Napa has to offer! There are so many wonderful wineries to visit! Grayson has created a list of our favorites over the course of our visits, check that out on the Recommendations tab. I suggest making an appointment as soon as you know which you would like to visit!

Buses will pick guests up from Churchhill Manor and Andaz Napa for the rehearsal dinner at 5:00 p.m. sharp. The rehearsal dinner will take place at Trefethen Family Vineyards from 6:00-9:30 p.m. All are welcome and attire is casual.

Cocktail attire.

RECOMMENDATIONS

Outpost
If you're up for a drive and don't mind windy roads, you will be glad you made the trek. Amazing winery, views, and juice. www.outpostwines.com

Round Pond
Great olive oil and wine tasting available, beautiful property. www.roundpond.com

Swanson
Salon tasting, good wine, casual experience, boutique feel. www.swansonvineyards.com

Frog’s Leap
Very, very casual and a neat place to stop by. Back patio tasting and pretty good juice. www.frogsleap.com

Corison
Amazing wine at affordable prices. Located right off St. Helena Hwy. www.corison.com

Mumm
Bubbly! Ansel Adams art gallery; Great start to the day! www.mummnapa.com

Domaine Carneros
#1 Champagne spot in Napa, enough said! www.domainecarneros.com

Hall Rutherford
Great juice, incredible estate, one of our favorites! www.hallwines.com

Gott’s Roadside
Amazing burger and sweet potato fries. Napa staple and great for the morning after too many tastings www.gotts.com

Boon Fly
Great breakfast spot, mini donuts and the best bloody mary and eggs benedict I've ever had! www.thecarnerosinn.com

Oxbow Public Market
Great place to find snacks, walk around. Napa’s version of an Eatzis www.oxbowpublicmarket.com

Mustards
Fancy Rib joint with way too many wines. Great American comfort food, we highly suggest the place. www.mustardsgrill.com

Oneotri
Brunch, lunch and dinner. Reservations needed. Very cool atmosphere and great food. www.oenotri.com

Reddwood
G’s favorite spot. Pizza and the likes. www.redd-wood.com
