ACCOMODATIONS

Napa has so much to offer, that we didn’t want to limit our guests to one specific lodging spot. From quaint bed and breakfasts, to 5 star hotels, there is something to fit everyone’s taste and budget. Although we want you to freely choose your accommodations, we are encouraging all guests to stay in, or near, Downtown Napa due to wedding events. If there is one thing I can stress on this website it is BOOK EARLY. We are getting married during peak season in Napa. Which means hotels, flights, etc. book extremely far in advance. Majority of hotels do not charge you until 60 days out, and simply need a credit card to reserve the room. If you are able to join us for this weekend, please go ahead and make reservations ASAP so that you are not left without a flight or hotel.
